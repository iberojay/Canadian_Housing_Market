# Canadian Housing Market 

**Decision:**
"Which Canadian cities are becoming infeasible to buy in for a mdeaian earner, and is it driven more by rates or prices?"

**Audience:**
First-time buyers/ fintech / policy analyst

**North-star metric:**
Mortgage payment as % of after-tax income (PTI)

**Success criteria:**
A stranger can watch a 3-minute walkthrough and understand:
- Top 5 least affordable CMAs
- What cause it (rates vs prices vs income)
- What action you recommend
